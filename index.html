Welcome to Texas hold 'em

Texas hold 'em is the most widely played variant of poker, the most popular card game in the world. It has grown from a regional card game into a global phenomenon, played at kitchen tables, casinos, online platforms, and major tournaments. It's a game of luck, skill and deception. Like all our greatest games, it's easy to learn and hard to master. The rules are simple, but the strategic possibilities are endless.

Where to start?

Every expert player started as a beginner, and the first step is always the same: understanding how the game works. With just a handful of rules and a clear view of the game's structure and flow, anyone can get started. Master the basics, and the rest of the game begins to unfold naturally.
