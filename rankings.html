Poker Hand Rankings

Poker hand rankings determine which hand wins at showdown. Texas hold 'em uses a standard hierarchy, starting from the strongest possible hand (Royal Flush) down to the weakest (High Card). As a new player, becoming familiar with these rankings is essential, since they guide every decision you make at the table.

Royal Flush

Ten to the ace of the same suit

Straight flush

Five consecutive cards of the same suit

Four of a Kind

Four cards of the same rank

Full house

Three of a kind and a pair

Flush

Five cards of the same suit

Straight

Five cards of consecutive ranks

Three of a Kind

Three cards of the same rank

Two-pair

Two different pairs

Pair

Two cards of the same rank

High card

No pair or combination; the highest card plays
